# Product Identity Uses One Complete Mascot

Debrute uses the same undivided full-body mascot as its mascot, brand mark, Web
and Desktop product icon, Dock icon, and Runtime tray icon. Platform renditions
may change dimensions, transparency, background, file format, or required color
treatment, but they preserve the complete character and never crop to the head,
remove the tiny body, or recompose its features. This favors consistent
cross-surface identity and the character's deliberately tiny-body proportions
over the conventional small-size strategy of extracting a separate simplified
head mark; each platform rendition must solve legibility without changing the
subject.
